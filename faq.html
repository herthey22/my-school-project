What is the admission process for students with special needs?

What types of special needs does your school cater to?
We cater to a range of special needs, including but not limited to autism spectrum disorder, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD),hearing disorder, learning disabilities, physical disabilities, and speech and language disorders.
What kind of support services do you offer for students with special needs?
We provide individualized education plans, speech and language therapy, occupational therapy, behavior intervention, counseling, and assistive technology support, among other services to meet the unique needs of each student.
How do you involve parents in their child's education and development?
We encourage active involvement of parents through regular communication, parent-teacher meetings, workshops, and support groups to ensure a collaborative approach to each student's education and development.
How are the classrooms and facilities adapted to meet the needs of students with special needs?
Our classrooms and facilities are equipped with specialized equipment, sensory areas, and modified learning materials to create an inclusive and supportive learning environment for our students with special needs.
Do you provide transportation for students with special needs?
We offer transportation services for students with special needs, ensuring safe and reliable transportation to and from the school.
